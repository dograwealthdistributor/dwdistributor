What is Retirement Income Calculator?

This calculator helps you estimate the total investment amount required to generate your desired monthly income during retirement using a safe withdrawal strategy. It is based on the commonly used 4% safe withdrawal rule.

How to use Retirement Income Calculator?

  • Enter the monthly income you want after retirement
  • The calculator uses a safe withdrawal rate (4%)
  • It will show the total corpus you may need to build
  • This helps you plan your retirement target clearly

Why use this Calculator?

  • Helps set a clear retirement goal
  • Shows how much wealth you need to create
  • Supports long-term financial planning
  • Makes retirement planning simple and practical
