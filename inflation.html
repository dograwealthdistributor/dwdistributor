What is Inflation?

Inflation reduces the purchasing power of money over time. This means the value of your money decreases if it does not grow faster than inflation.

How to use Inflation Calculator?

  • Enter your investment amount
  • Enter expected return rate
  • Enter time period
  • Adjust inflation rate (default 6%)

Why is Inflation Important?

Understanding inflation helps you make better investment decisions and ensures your wealth grows in real terms, not just nominal value.
